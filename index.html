Experience authentic therapeutic massage with VNHands.

Welcome to VNHands self help healing.This is where the tagline of the business will go along with the loction and a background image

My name is Thi Kim Ngan Tran. I have over 8 years of experience in therapeutic massage including such as deep tissue,relaxation, reflexology and Easten methods. Before moving to the UK, I work as a massage therapist in Viet Nam, where I build strong and trusted relationships with my clients. They appreciated my care,skill and personalised treatment.

Now I’m living in Swansea, I want to bring this same high-quality,healing service to my local community. I believe massage is not only for relaxation but also for improving health and well-being. I have named my brand VNHands ,which reflects both my Vietnamese roots and the healing work I do with my hands.

In addition to formal qualifications and continuous professtional development, I’m currently volunteering as a massage therapist at Swansea Care Centre. This experience allows I to support the people in community while further developing my skill and demonstrating a strong commitment to care and service.

VNHands targets working professionals, new mothers, and older adults in Swansea. The business aims to build a loyal client base through excellent customer care, community engagement, and effective local marketing. The long-term vision includes expanding services to include pregnancy massage and mobile treatments.
